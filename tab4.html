How is your government doing?


The United Nations (a big international organisation that helps governments talk and work together) publishes a ranking on how well governments are doing in their digital government work. Have a look at the chart below and see if you can find yours.


Interested in knowing more about the ranking?


The ranking is calculated based on three indexes that measure how effective your country is in deliverying public services online. The indexes are Online Service Index(OSI), Telecommunications Infrastructure index (TII), and Human Capital Index(HCI). The OSI assesses how people like you can easily find and use the online services offered by your government. The TII checks the number of internet users, mobile and landline subscriptions. And the HCI minaly takes into account the average education level and literacy rate in your country.
