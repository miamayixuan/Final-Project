How the World Bank has spent its money


$80 billion is a lot of money - and that’s just what the World Bank has spent. The overall amount spent worldwide is likely to be much larger. So what’s all that money being spent on? If you look at the word cloud below, you’ll see some words are bigger than others. If they’re big, it means the World Bank has spent its $80 billion on many such projects. If they’re smaller, it means fewer such projects have been financed by the World Bank.



Amount spent by project category (in million USD)
