What on?

World Bank Project

$80 billion is a lot of money - and that’s just what the World Bank has spent. The overall amount spent worldwide is likely to be much larger. So what’s all that money being spent on? If you look at the word cloud below, you’ll see some words are bigger than others. If they’re big, it means the World Bank has spent lots of its $80 billion on that thing. If they’re smaller, it means less has been spent on it.

World Bank projects approved for fiscal years 1991 to 2021

Commonly used words are larger and slightly faded in color. Less common words are smaller and darker.
